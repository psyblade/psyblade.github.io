Lesson 7: Pixel Coordinates

A pixel on the screen is describe by a set of coordinates which is its location on the x and y axis.

To plot a pixel, you need to determine its x and y coordinates.

STEP 1: Hover your mouse to where you want to draw your dot on the X axis and click on it.

STEP 2: Hover your mouse to where you want to draw your dot on the Y axis and click on it.
