Setup

Open template or new file in Microstation

Close Third Window

Make sure top left drop down box shows "Drawing"

Go to view Attirbute at the top left corner button of View 1 (Window 1) Panel

Turn off grid and turn on ACS triad

Do the same for View 2 (Window 2)

In the top right corner of the app window in the search bar, search for preferences

Under the input category in the preference window, check "ESC Exit Command"

Location of Drawing Tools

Arcs and circle under Home Tab > Placement Panel

Move Movments

For movement (rotate view) SHIFT and Press Mouse Scroll Button

To Pan on the XY Plane, Just press the mouse scroll button

Changing Views

To change view of Window 1, go to window 1 , toolbar button with 3 Arrows called Rotate View. Click it or click and hold onto the button (for more than 1 second) for options.

Select Isometric View for Window 1

Select Top View for Window 2

Layer Manager

Goto Home tab > Primary panel > click on 3rd button (top-down) to the right of "Attach Tools" button

In Level Manager window, right click any where on blank screen and create new layer. Name it GLASS, GRASS, FLOOR TILE and set color

Copy Objects

From the home tab > Manipulate panel, click on copy

In the copy window, type how many copies you want.

Select the object you want to copy and drag it to either - left/right or

up/down

You can click on the bottom X:Y:Z value box to adjust the distance

Selecting

Press CTRL and click to select multiple objects

Cutting with shapes

Select Home Tab > Modify Panel > Trim Multiple.

Select Cutting Shape, then Select Target Shape. After that MUST right click to FINISH.

Then click on the outline of the target shape and click on the cutting shape again to get the result lines. It seems you have to click on the outlines a few times, like A, B, B, A

Extra Wide Camera (Not important, just for better camera view)

To change view of Window 1, go to window 1 , toolbar button with 3D box.

Click it or click and hold onto the button (for more than 1 second) for options.

In the Perspective View Toolbar button in your work windows, Select extra wide camera

Once you have selected wide Camera you, you may not see your building. Click on "Fit View" toolbar button on your work window to zoom back into view to see your building

Extrude

Top Left Corner of App Window, current at "Drawing", select "Modelling" then click the "Solids" tab. Then in the "Creat Solids" panel, select "Extrude"

Extruding Surfaces and the Floor

Go to Worms Eye View

Select the Floor Blocks

We are going to Extract the Surface of these block by going to Modelling UI > Solids Tab, and Solid Utilities Panel (top right icon)

Click on it and an Extract Window will pop up. In the drop down box, select "Faces". Originally it was "Edges"

Select one bottom surface of the block at a time and click on the surface 3 times (First time it is highlighted by solid yellow, then a dotted yellow, click on it again for the third time.

Drawing Curves Lines

go to Drawing UI > Home Tab > Placement Panel, 2 buttons below the the circle, click on the cuve toolbar button.

Select the curves and then in Drawing UI > Home Tab > Groups Panel, Select "Create Complex Shape" toolbar button which is middle row and mid column button.

You Can Skip This Section

ASC Triad

To manipulate/move ASC, go to "Drawing Aids" tab > ACS Panel > click on move ASC Triad

Creating Smart Lines

Click on multi-snap on the Snap Toolbar at the bottom bar of the app window.

Right click on the same multi-snap button, and select settings.

Make sure Intersectin, Midpoint, Keypoint, Nearest are checked.

Go to Home Tab > Placement Panel, select place smartline. Place line on to

ASC and drag to make verticle line.

Press CTRL-tab to bring up property window and click on line to show properties.

You can set the length of the line from there.

Draw a verticle line and horizontal line
