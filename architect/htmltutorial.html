Download VSCode

Install VSCode

Click and Run the VSCode File You Downloaded

Open the Explorer Window in VSCode

When you select "explorer" a window will open to the right of VSCode showing the files in your work folder

Open Your Browser Side By Side With Your VSCode

You can do this by dragging your VSCode window all the way to the left end of your screen. This will automatically resize your VSCode window to half the screen.

Open your favorite browser and do the same thing. Drag it to the right end of your screen so that it will automatically resize and align to the right of your screen.

Now click on the "Lesson1.html" file from your VSCode explorer window to edit it in VSCode.

You can also drag the "index.html" file from your VSCode explorer window into the browser to open it.
