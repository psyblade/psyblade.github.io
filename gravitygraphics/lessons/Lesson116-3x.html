Exercise: Conical Chord Diagram

Reproduce the conical chord diagram pattern you see on the screen.

The chords are drawn on a flat, circular surface but give the illusion of a three-dimensional conical shape through the arrangement of intersecting lines and curves. The result can look like a conic section (ellipse, parabola, or hyperbola) depending on how the points on the circle are connected and the way the lines are curved or angled.
