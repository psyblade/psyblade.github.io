Lesson 58: Modulus Operator

The modulus operator (%) in JavaScript is used to find the remainder of a division operation between two numbers.

remainder = dividend % divisor;

For example:

4 % 3 = 1 // 4/3 = 1 with a remainder of 1.
9 % 4 = 1 // 9/4 = 2 with a remainder of 1.
15 % 5 = 0 // 15/5 = 3 with no remainder, 0.
20 % 6 = 2 // 20/6 = 3 with a remainder of 2.
11 % 7 = 4 // 11/7 = 1 with a remainder of 4.
