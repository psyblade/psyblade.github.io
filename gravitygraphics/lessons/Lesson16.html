Lesson 16: Random Number

Instead of picking a random position for our dot ourselves, we can also have the computer do it for us.

For that, we use the command random() in place of our x and y position values, like this:

plot(random(0,59), random(0,39), "lime");

When we specify a range like (0,59), we are asking the computer to pick a number anywhere from 0 to 59 for us.
