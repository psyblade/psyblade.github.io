Lesson 50: Health Bar

Now let's visualize percentage with a health bar. First, let's draw a health bar.

gr();
rect(5, 14, 54, 24, "lime");
rect(6, 15, 53, 23, "green");
rect(6, 15, 30, 23, "cyan");

You will see the length of the inner cyan bar that represents our current health is 30 - 6 from the x-axis. So if we have 50% of our health, that would be (30-6) * 0.5.

Try to alter the current health in the code accordingly.
