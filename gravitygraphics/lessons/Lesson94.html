Lesson: Two Dimension Arrays

When we add an array within array, it becomes a two-dimension array. Here we added the price to our fruits array.

let array_fruits = [
    ["apple", 1.50], 
    ["orange", 0.80], 
    ["banana", 0.60],
    ["blueberry", 3.00], 
    ["lime", 0.50], 
    ["coconut", 2.50]
];

Here's how we access the name and price of the fruits

var fruit1_name = array_fruits[0][0];
var fruit1_color = array_fruits[0][1]
print(fruit1_name + " is " + fruit1_color);
